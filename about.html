We invest early, with conviction

We invest almost exclusively in the United States and Canada, though we make exceptions for certain natively global industries.

primary

Primary investments are 95% of our capital allocation. We focus on the stage of the company, not the name of the round. As a result, we lead or co-lead rounds between $5-20M with an initial investment of $3-8M with capital reserved for follow-on investments.

SEED

Seed investments make up the remainder of our allocation. While we will, on occasion, end up leading a larger Seed round, we usually find ourselves participating in the round with an initial check size of $250k. For these investments, we do not take board seats.

Why RRE

We believe that success isn’t something that just happens, it’s something that can be made to happen again and again despite changing economic cycles, technology waves, and market opportunities. At RRE, we pursue great outcomes — not by building companies, but by empowering the visionaries who dare to build them.
