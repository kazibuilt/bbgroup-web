It is with deep sadness that we announce the passing of our Partner and Co-founder, James D. Robinson III.

While Jim is well known for his time as Chairman and CEO of American Express and his service as a Director of Coca-Cola, Bristol Myers Squibb and other leading Global companies, what is less well known is his work as a venture capitalist. For almost 30 years as an RRE General Partner, Jim mentored a younger generation of entrepreneurs, CEOs, and investors. He was captivated by the power of technology to enable new businesses and drive progress in the corporate world. He leaves behind a legacy of great companies and the firm he co-founded with his son, Jim Robinson IV, and Stuart Ellman.
