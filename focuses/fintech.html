Perspective

While this process has not reached its conclusion, we will eventually see a return to bundled services and platforms that rebundle the best of the best solutions into a unified provider. Meanwhile, many non-fintech companies, worn down by the lethargy and friction of securing services (or their customers securing services), are themselves becoming financial institutions of a sort.
