Jason is a General Partner at RRE. He joined the team in 2014 as an Analyst and is the firm's fastest promoted investor and youngest General Partner. In 2019, he was recognized on the Forbes 30 Under 30 Venture Capital list. In addition to his RRE responsibilities, Jason also serves as the Co-Director of the NYC VC—New York’s largest venture group that organizes networking and educational events for the growing NYC ecosystem. Jason leads the firm's investments in crypto, B2B fintech, healthcare, developer tools, infrastructure platforms, industrial internet of things, and anything in traditional enterprise software. Prior to joining RRE, Jason worked at Microsoft, where he developed Office’s first dedicated startup analysis pipeline. Jason holds a B.A. from Harvard University where he studied Psychology and Computer Science. While there, Jason founded the Harvard College Tech Review, where he served as Editor-in-Chief, worked as a freelance designer and photographer, and co-founded two student startups.
